# Linktank AI Readiness Assessment

Interactive AI readiness assessment tool for Independent Financial Adviser (IFA) practices in South Africa.

## Features
- 29 scored questions across 6 weighted domains
- Live progress tracking
- Auto-generated readiness report with animated score dial
- Domain breakdown with traffic light risk summary
- Personalised next steps based on readiness band
- Print-to-PDF export
- Fully client-side — no backend required

## Domains
1. Data Environment & Governance (25%)
2. Technology Infrastructure (20%)
3. Regulatory & Compliance Awareness (20%)
4. Business Process & Use Case Clarity (15%)
5. People & Culture (10%)
6. Vendor & Budget Readiness (10%)
